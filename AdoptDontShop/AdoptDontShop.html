A phrase Worth Thousands of Lives

Have you ever heard of the phrase "Adopt Don't Shop?" Adopt Don't Shop is a campaign slogan that is used to promote adopting pets from shelters rather than breeders/puppy mills


Why to Adopt

Dogs from puupy mills and commercial-breeding facilities live in filthy conditions without adequate food, water or veterinary care. Practices and conditions in most puppy mills are unethical and downright despicable in many cases. Female dogs are bred at every opportunity with little to no recovery time between litters. By purchasing these dogs, the puppy mills and breeding facilities are only making more money to contiue their businesses.



More Benefits of Adopting

  • You'll save a life.
  • You'll get a great pet.
  • It will cost less.
  • It's one way to fight puppy mills.
  • Adoption helps more than one animal.


It's as easy as 1,2,3

The Adoption Process

  1. Find your new four legged friend.
  2. Go to the shelter to see the pet.
  3. Tell a staff member your interested
  4. Fill out the adoption application
  5. Once you get the all clear you can take your new pet home!
